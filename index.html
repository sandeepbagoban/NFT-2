Japan,
January 1877

Following the ban on the wearing of the sword for samurai, Seisan Senso broke out, opposing the last samurai and the new imperial government.

To Saigo Takamori, this ban was a disrespectful measure against the samurai and their bushido.

He put his samurai battalion in combat order, and he undertook to besiege the imperial garrison of the fortress of Kumamoto.

But the government send some 300,000 conscript soldiers to the island and took advantage.

After six weeks of fierce fighting, Saigo and his battalion succumbed…

Japan,
January 3877

2000 years later, in 3877, the world that has totally gone to hell, cybernetics and contamination made half the planet inhabitable and technology destroyed human interactions.

However, if there is something that has not changed : it is control and the power of the imperial army.

The same family rules over Japan with the wealthiest, in front of the unhealthy steampunk districts.

Before his disparition, our hero of 1877, Saigo Takamori left a little something : a kid.

Following this defeat, this secret was well kept and the girl took the family name Takahashi to avoid any suspicion.

Isamu Takahashi

2000 years later, Isamu Takahashi, a young student engineer, discovers by chance who was her ancestor, a certain Saigo Takamori.

To honor her heritage, and the tradition of fighting for her freedom, she must come out of the shadows.

But alone, she can do nothing.

So, are you ready to become a samurai?
